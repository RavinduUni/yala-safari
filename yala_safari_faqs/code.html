Best Time to Visit

When is the best time to spot leopards? expand_more

The dry season, from May to August, is widely considered the best time for leopard sightings. As water holes dry up, animals congregate around the remaining water sources, making them easier to spot. However, Yala is beautiful year-round, and leopards are seen frequently even in the greener months.

Is the park open all year? expand_more

Generally, yes. However, Block 1 is sometimes closed for a few weeks in September or October for maintenance and drought relief for the animals. We recommend checking our live updates before booking during these months.

Did you know?

The Leopard Kingdom

Yala National Park has one of the highest leopard densities in the world, with approximately one leopard per square kilometer in Block 1.

Safari Logistics

How long is a typical safari? expand_more

We offer three main options:

  • Morning Safari: 6:00 AM – 10:00 AM (Best for birds & bears)
  • Afternoon Safari: 2:30 PM – 6:30 PM (Best for leopards)
  • Full Day: 6:00 AM – 6:30 PM (For the serious enthusiast)
What kind of vehicle will we use? expand_more

All safaris are conducted in modified 4x4 Jeeps with elevated seating and open sides for optimal viewing and photography. Each jeep accommodates up to 6 guests comfortably. Private jeeps can be booked for exclusivity.

Safety & Rules

Is it safe for children? expand_more

Yes, but children must remain seated inside the jeep at all times. We recommend safaris for children aged 5 and above due to the bumpy terrain and need for silence.

Can we feed the animals? expand_more

Strictly no. Feeding animals disrupts their natural diet and behavior. It is also dangerous and illegal within the park.

What to Bring

What clothing is best? expand_more

Wear neutral, earth-toned colors (Khaki, Green, Brown) to blend in. Bright colors can startle animals. Lightweight, breathable fabrics are recommended.

Camera gear checklist? expand_more

A zoom lens (at least 300mm) is ideal. Bring extra batteries and memory cards. A bean bag for stability on the jeep rail is often better than a tripod.

Booking & Policies

Cancellation Policy

Full refund for cancellations made 72 hours prior to the scheduled safari. 50% refund for cancellations between 24-72 hours. No refunds for same-day cancellations.

Payment Methods

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex) through our secure online portal. Cash payments are accepted at the office in LKR or USD.
